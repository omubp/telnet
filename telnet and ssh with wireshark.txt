Examining a Telnet Session with Wireshark.

1. open wireshark in soc
2. select loopback.io in wireshark
3. open terminal and enter telnet localhost
4. apply display filter telnet
5. Right-click one of the Telnet lines in the Packet list, select Follow > TCP Stream.

Examine an SSH Session with Wireshark
1 & 2 same as above
3. open terminal and enter ssh localhost
4. apply display filter ssh
5. Right-click one of the SSHv2 lines in the Packet list section of Wireshark, and in the drop-down list, select the Follow > TCP Stream
